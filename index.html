More About Me

Michael Beaujean is a web developer and creative technologist based in New York City. He's helped launch the web presence of large marketing campaigns as well as re-brands and creative emendments. Michael's attention to detail is reflected in his engineering of elegant interfaces as well as clean, cohesive code. Constantly striving to grow and learn, he makes it a point to research, learn and experiment with the latest in web design and development. When coding, Michael enjoys drinking coffee and Spotify. When not coding, he enjoys good beer & playing his guitar. He currently resides in Greenpoint, Brooklyn.
